Pj 1. Lost & Found

REFLECTION

My partner described her object 'It’s a blue fabric tea bag that hangs from a pink thread. Its materiality is soft yet rough to the touch. It was thin but squishy and had little density. Its blue fabric glistened yellow and silver in the light. Atop the front surface was a small frog, who’s gaze seemed comforting and learned. Although once pristine, his light green and blue had only slightly faded into the gray of use.'

My interpretation was 'a lightblue triangle with few pink strokes on the top The base color is lightblue with some yellow and silver colors mixed In the middle of the triangle, there is a frog with a comforting face. Its color is lightgreen with blue and grey mixed.'

At first, I started with filling the background. I wanted to give a sunlight effect, I gave a slight gradient of brown. Then I drew the main lightblue triangle. I wanted to use triangle(), but realized that I can not change the angle of the corner for triangle. Since I wanted the tea bag to have soft texture, I decided to use curve() instead. I had to overlap two triangles to create the shadow. On the tea bag, I gave texture and additional colors of the tea bag using ellipse and strokes with overlay.

For the thread tied on the bag, I also used curve() to give gentle curves to the threads. The frog printed on the middle of the bag is made of ellipse() with translate() and rotate() to make the face. I used arc() for the eyes and curve() for the mouth in order to express the comforting emotion. Then I gave fading effect using some ellipse() of blue and lightgreen on the frog.

I also wanted to make an animation of the tea bag swinging, but because I couldn't group the objects in p5js, I had to give up that part. Instead, I gave animation to the sunlight. I first created the light by drawing a big triangle with blendMode(Screen). Then I added an animation of changing the alpha channel percentage between 79 and 150.
