Pj 2. Face Generater

REFLECTION

When I was creating this drawing, I started by drawing the simplist form of the cat's face with circles, triangles, and arc. Then I gave change to the size by making the elements interact with my mouse. I set the concept as the cat being surprized at night because cat's pupil gets bigger when dark. Therefore I made the background turn from bright yellow to dark blue. Also, I used if else statement for the eyes so that the colors would change randomly whenever I press the mouse. The hardest part of this assignment was to place every elements in where I want them to be and to make them change in the way I want them to change. I had to arrange all the numbers one by one. Through this project, I learned that it is very important to plan everything out before start making the actual drawing because it is hard to make things move in the way I want them to move.
