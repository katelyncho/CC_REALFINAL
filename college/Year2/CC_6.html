Pj 6. Data Profile

REFLECTION

For this project, I created a data portrait of the music playlist I listened to for a week because I am a person who always have to be listening to something whenever I focus. I recorded what I listen to every time I played a music and categorized them into 6 big sections. My original plan was to make a one big circular sine wave divided into 6, however, I could not figure out how to make it. Therefore I had to change the plan. I created six different sine waves and partially covered them with arc. Each sine waves represent each category of the music genre, and they are cut by the percentage I listen to. As I wrote on the bottom left, the sine waves are divided into two big categories of lyrics and no lyrics by red and blue colors.

I also added mouseIsPressed statement so that the text for the categories are not shown at first but appears when the mouse is pressed.

I was able to enjoy the process of this project because it was my first time actually trying to calculate which music I listen most to. Even though my original plan did not work, I was able to understand better about sine wave in the process of trying to fix the problem.
