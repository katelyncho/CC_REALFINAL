Pj 3. Experimental Clock

REFLECTION

Break would mean different for everyone, and for me, it means going back home, which is 15hour flight away from here. As much as I like studying here, I also always want to go back home and see my family and friends. Therefore I made a clock that counts the d-day for break. I set the d-day as December 14th because that is when my flight is booked.

My other two design ideas were tracking when I cook and do duolingo. However, because they were so irregular, I chose this clock. It was challenging for me discovering how to make the clock go backwards, which is opposite from normal clocks. I had some trials and errors that even made the whole website freeze whenever I run the code. However, I was able to figure out a better way to create same result. Through this project, I learned how to control the speed of the animation in the way I want it to look like.

If i could do this project again, I would make the percentage bar in the shape of a plane and make it fly away when the percentage become 100%.
